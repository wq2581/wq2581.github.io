关于我

I am a PhD student in BMI at UF, advised by Prof. Qianqian Song. My research interests include AI and its applications in biomedical field.

我是佛罗里达大学生物医学信息学系的博士生，导师是宋乾乾教授。我的研究兴趣包括人工智能及其在生物医学领域的应用。

Before joining the University of Florida, I received my bachelor's degree in Internet of Things Engineering from Zhejiang Wanli University in June 2021, where my thesis advisor was Professor Jiong Shi. I also obtained a master's degree in Computer Science from Zhejiang Normal University in June 2024, under the supervision of Professor Jia Zhu. During this time, as a member of the Zhejiang Key Laboratory of Intelligent Education Technology and Application team, I also received guidance from Professor Changqin Huang and Professor Ming Li. In the summer of 2023, I visited Professor Daniel E. Acuna's lab in the Computer Science Department at CU Boulder.

在加入佛罗里达大学之前，我在浙江万里学院获得物联网工程学士学位，导师是施炯教授。之后在浙江师范大学获得计算机科学硕士学位，导师是朱佳教授。在此期间，作为浙江省智能教育技术与应用重点实验室成员，我还受到黄昌勤教授和李明教授的指导。2023年夏天，我访问了科罗拉多大学博尔德分校计算机系Daniel E. Acuna教授的实验室。

自2022年以来，我的研究成果已发表17篇论文，其中包括7篇以第一作者或共同第一作者身份发表在《Nature Communications》、《Advanced Science》、《Briefings in Bioinformatics》和《Bioinformatics》等国际顶尖期刊上的文章。这些论文累计被引用超过170次，h指数为7，i10指数为5。我曾担任30个以上国际期刊和会议的同行评审员，迄今已完成超过50次同行评审工作。
